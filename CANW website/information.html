Our action are just as important as words.

  1. Empower people to make a difference in the lives of others.
  2. Transform: Communicate the transformative impact of donations and support.
  3. Change: Highlight the power of positive change that contributions can bring about.

  • Fostering and Adoption: CANW has fostering and adoption services, it's the placement of children in caring and supportive environments. This includes recruiting and supporting foster carers and adoptive families.
  • Mental Health Support: Recognizing the importance of mental health, CANW offers support and resources for children and families dealing with mental health challenges.
  • Community Engagement: CANW often engage with local communities to address specific needs.
  • Training and Support: For services such as fostering and adoption, charities often provide training and ongoing support for caregivers to ensure the well-being of the children, so with this we are always training our staff to make sure they are doing everything right to make everything perfect for you.
